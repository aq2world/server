* Description *

Sector 7 is a restricted industrial processing zone deep inside a classified facility.

Dominated by towering containment columns filled with unstable green coolant, the map centers around a multi-level core chamber surrounded by control rooms, cargo platforms, and exposed catwalks. Tight corridors feed into open vertical combat spaces, creating constant pressure between elevation control and ground movement.

Upper walkways provide strong sightlines across the reactor floor, while lower routes allow for fast flanks and surprise pushes. Crates and structural pillars break visibility just enough to reward positioning without turning fights into random chaos.

Sector 7 is built for aggressive, fast-paced Action Quake play where controlling height and timing rotations decides the round.
